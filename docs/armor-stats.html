# Get Armor Stats By Armor

Retrieve information about armor stats by armor.

  • Method: GET
  • Endpoint: https://api.openalbion.com/api/v2/armor-stats/armor/{armor_id}

Example Request:

GET https://api.openalbion.com/api/v2/armor-stats/armor/1
